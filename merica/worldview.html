Worldview

I am a Christian. I believe that Jesus Christ came and died for our sins. We are all sinners, but because of Christ's grace, we are set free and reconciled with God. God is the ultimate creator. He created the universe. I also believe that as followers of Christ we should imitate Christ and spread the Good News. When we die, if we are believers, we will go to Heaven where we spend our eternal life with God.
